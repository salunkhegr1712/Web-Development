JavaScript Comparation Operators

==	    equal to
===	    equal value and equal type
!=	    not equal
!==	    not equal value or not equal type
>	      greater than
<	      less than
>=	    greater than or equal to
<=	    less than or equal to
?	      ternary operator

JavaScript Logical Operators

&&	     logical and
||	     logical or
!	       logical not
